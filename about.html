I am a Geographic Information Science (GIS) professional and University of Minnesota graduate with experience in geospatial analysis, cartography, spatial data management, and infrastructure mapping. Through this role, I have gained experience working with enterprise GIS systems, maintaining spatial datasets, and supporting engineering and operations teams through accurate geographic information.

My experience includes working with ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, spatial analysis techniques, cartographic design, GPS data collection, and web-based GIS applications. I also have programming experience with Python and exposure to geospatial data workflows that integrate analysis, visualization, and decision-making.

My interests include transportation systems, infrastructure management, urban planning, web GIS development, and emerging geospatial technologies. I am particularly interested in how spatial data can improve decision-making, increase operational efficiency, and help organizations better understand the geographic patterns that influence their work.
